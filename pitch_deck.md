# Helpeez Pitch Deck Framework
*Use this draft to build your presentation slides and script for the 3-minute video presentation.*

---

## Slide 1: Title Slide
### **Daily household help, without the daily stress.**
*   **Subtitle:** Subscription-based daily domestic help with 100% guaranteed backup support.
*   **Presenter Note:** Start with energy! "Good morning judges, we are Helpeez, and we are solving the most common morning crisis in Indian households."

---

## Slide 2: The Problem
### **The Chaotic Morning Routine**
*   **The Pain Point:** Domestic helpers taking sudden, unannounced leaves causes high stress, missed meetings for working professionals, and immense burden on mothers.
*   **Market Fragmentation:** The daily domestic help market (sweeping, cooking, washing utensils) is completely unorganized. Local agencies are offline, and premium cleaning services are too expensive for daily use.
*   **Presenter Note:** "Imagine waking up at 7:30 AM to a text saying your maid isn't coming. Your entire morning routine, breakfast, and work calls are thrown into chaos. This is a daily reality for 100 million households in India."

---

## Slide 3: The Solution
### **Introducing Helpeez: Organized & Reliable Help**
*   **Guaranteed Backup System:** If your primary helper takes a leave, our dispatch algorithm automatically sends a verified backup helper at your exact scheduled slot.
*   **Professional Standards:** Background & police-verified helpers, standard pricing, and professional training.
*   **Flat Subscription:** Simple monthly billing with zero awkward daily negotiations.
*   **Presenter Note:** "Helpeez is the peace-of-mind subscription for your home. We manage the helpers so you don't have to. And if someone takes a holiday, our automated backup dispatches a standby helper instantly."

---

## Slide 4: How It Works
### **Simple, Tech-Enabled Operations**
1.  **Select & Estimate:** Select the exact daily chores you need and get a customized monthly quote.
2.  **Smart Matching:** Our algorithm matches you with the best helper based on your preferred time slots.
3.  **Live Tracking:** Know exactly when your helper is arriving.
4.  **Instant Backup Alert:** If a helper is sick, the system registers the leave and dispatches the nearest on-duty backup helper instantly.
*   **Presenter Note:** "Showcase our web app prototype here! Walk them through the subscription estimator and run the leave simulation to show the auto-dispatch system in action."

---

## Slide 5: Market Opportunity
### **A Massive, Under-tapped Market**
*   **Target Market:** Urban middle-class, dual-income households, and elderly families living alone in Tier 1 & Tier 2 Indian cities.
*   **Market Size:** Over 100 Million addressable households.
*   **Market Need:** 90% of urban households rely on domestic help daily, yet 0% of the daily help market is consolidated under a single national brand.
*   **Presenter Note:** "The daily chores market is highly recurring. People don't unsubscribe from daily cleaning or cooking; it is a permanent necessity."

---

## Slide 6: Business Model
### **Highly Recurring Unit Economics**
*   **Monthly Subscription:** Average order value of ₹2,000 - ₹5,000 per month depending on chores.
*   **Commission Margin:** 20-25% gross margin on services while paying helpers higher wages than local market rates.
*   **High Retention:** Daily-use services build long-term sticky habits, yielding very high customer lifetime value (LTV).
*   **Presenter Note:** "Because our service is built on reliability, our retention rate will naturally outclass unorganized local markets where helpers leave unexpectedly."

---

## Slide 7: Social Impact
### **Empowering the Domestic Workforce**
*   **Dignity of Labor:** Standardized working hours, biometrics, and a professionalized career ladder.
*   **Financial Inclusion:** Consistent digital monthly salaries, bank accounts, and health insurance benefits.
*   **Safety & Trust:** Background checks protect both the household and the helpers.
*   **Presenter Note:** "We aren't just making lives easier for families; we are formalizing and bringing dignity to one of India's largest unorganized workforces."
